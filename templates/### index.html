Introduction

Welcome to Algebra Basics! This is an interactive resource designed to help you learn the fundamentals of algebra. Whether you're a complete beginner or you're looking to brush up on your skills, you'll find everything you need here.

To get started, simply navigate to the Lessons page to begin learning. As you complete lessons, you'll be able to track your progress on the Progress page. Once you feel comfortable with the material, you can challenge yourself with the quizzes on the Quizzes page.

So what are you waiting for? Let's get started!
